Subject: Tip: use your personal email address

Dear {{name}},

You created an eduID using an institution's email address. Important: if you leave this institution, you'll likely lose that email address. This may also mean you lose access to your eduID. We therefore recommend using a personal email address for your eduID. You can change this in the settings of mijn.eduid.nl.

Kind regards,
